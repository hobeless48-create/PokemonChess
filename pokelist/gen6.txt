### 718 Zygarde Reassembly Unit ability
Type: Dragon/Ground
Url: https://assets.pokemon.com/assets/cms2/img/pokedex/detail/718.png
Role: Def
Cost: 6 HP: 16 Atk: 4 Def (In-Game): 3 (Stats: Def 121 + Sp.Def 95 = 216 → 220 → 220/100 = 2.2 → floor 2 + base 1 = 3)
EvoCost: 
EvoTo: Zygarde (10%)
EvoFrom:
Legendary: true HatchGroup: Legendary
Ability: Zygarde Reassembly Unit
A special structure called Zygarde Reassembly Unit is placed on the field when the battle starts.
At the end of each turn, every empty tile within AoE(3) around the Reassembly Unit has a 10% chance to generate a Cell of Zygarde.
Rules for Cell spawning:
- Cells cannot spawn on occupied tiles.
- Each tile rolls separately.
- Cells remain on the field until collected or destroyed.
Collecting Cells
- Allied Pokémon can attack a Cell to collect it.
- When collected, the Cell is added to the Reassembly Unit’s total Cell count.
- Enemy attacks on a Cell destroy it permanently instead of collecting it.
- If a Cell of Zygarde is destroyed by status effects, terrain effects, passive damage, or indirect damage, it does not count as collected.
Evolution Requirements
- 10 Cell of zygarde → Summon Zygarde 10%
- Zygarde 10% + 15 additional Cell of zygarde → Evolve into Zygarde 50%
- Zygarde 50% + 25 additional Cell of zygarde→ Evolve into Zygarde Complete Forme
- Cell of zygarde has Hp=1 def=0
Evolution Rules
- Zygarde forms can move, attack, and continue collecting Cells normally.
- Evolution happens automatically immediately after reaching the required Cell count.
- The current Zygarde unit transforms directly into the next form.
When evolving:
- Keep all buffs and debuffs.
- Keep the same board position.
- Keep total damage taken from previous forms.
Example:
Zygarde 10% has 8 Max HP.
It currently has 5 HP, meaning it has taken 3 damage.
When evolving into Zygarde 50% with 11 Max HP, it keeps the 3 damage taken.
Result: Zygarde 50% becomes 8/11 HP.
Complete Forme Rules
- When Zygarde Complete Forme appears:
   -The Reassembly Unit disappears permanently.
   -All remaining Cells on the battlefield disappear.
   -No more Cells can spawn for the rest of the battle.
Death Rules
If:   Zygarde 10% faints or Zygarde 50% faints
Then: That Zygarde form is permanently destroyed.
